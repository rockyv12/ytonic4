Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our pre-monetized channels

Is this process compliant with YouTube's terms of service?

Yes, channel ownership transfers are permitted under YouTube's terms of service. We ensure that all transfers are conducted in compliance with platform policies.

How long does the transfer process take?

The typical transfer process takes 24-48 hours from payment to complete handover. Premium package transfers can be expedited and completed within 24 hours.

Can I change the channel name and branding?

Yes, once the transfer is complete, you have full ownership and can update the channel name, logo, banner, and other branding elements to match your content.
