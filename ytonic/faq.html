Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pre-monetized YouTube channels, pricing, transfer process, and more.

General Questions

What is a pre-monetized YouTube channel?

A pre-monetized YouTube channel is one that has already met YouTube's monetization requirements (1,000+ subscribers and 4,000+ watch hours) and has been approved for the YouTube Partner Program. When you purchase a pre-monetized channel, you can start earning revenue immediately without waiting to meet these requirements yourself.

Is buying a pre-monetized channel allowed by YouTube?

Yes, YouTube allows channel ownership transfers. We ensure that all our transfers comply with YouTube's terms of service. The transfer process involves changing all account credentials and recovery options to ensure complete ownership transfer.

What types of channels do you offer?

We offer a variety of pre-monetized channels across different niches including gaming, lifestyle, tech reviews, educational content, and more. Each channel listing includes information about the content niche, subscriber count, watch hours, and monetization status.

Can I change the channel name and branding after purchase?

Yes, once the transfer is complete, you have full ownership of the channel and can update the name, logo, banner, and other branding elements to match your content style and niche.

Pricing & Payment

How much does a pre-monetized channel cost?

We offer two main pricing tiers:

  • Basic Package: $389 - Includes a channel with 1,000+ subscribers and 4,000+ watch hours
  • Premium Package: $449 - Includes a channel with 1,500+ subscribers, 4,000+ watch hours, and instant AdSense change option

We also offer aged channels for $20 additional fee, which have been established for a longer period and may have additional benefits.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrency. All payments are processed securely through our payment processor.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If the channel does not meet the specifications described in the listing or if there are issues with the transfer process that we cannot resolve, we will provide a full refund. Please refer to our refund policy for more details.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, the price you see is the price you pay. There are no hidden fees or additional charges. The package price includes the channel transfer, setup assistance, and post-purchase support.

Transfer Process

How long does the transfer process take?

The typical transfer process takes 24-48 hours from payment to complete handover. Premium package transfers can be expedited and completed within 24 hours in most cases.

How is the channel transferred to me?

The transfer process involves:

  1. Secure transfer of all account credentials
  2. Updating recovery email and phone number to your information
  3. Setting up two-factor authentication with your devices
  4. Verifying your access to all channel features and monetization settings
  5. Confirming successful transfer before finalizing the transaction

Our team guides you through each step to ensure a smooth and secure transfer.

Is my payment protected during the transfer?

Yes, your payment is held in escrow until the channel transfer is complete and verified. This ensures that you only pay once you have full control of the channel and are satisfied with the transfer.

What happens if there are issues during the transfer?

Our support team is available to resolve any issues that may arise during the transfer process. If we cannot resolve the issues to your satisfaction, we will provide a full refund.

Monetization & Earnings

How soon can I start earning after purchase?

You can start earning immediately after the transfer is complete. Since the channel is already approved for monetization, your new uploads will be eligible for ad revenue from day one.

What is the "instant AdSense change" option in the Premium package?

The instant AdSense change option allows you to connect your own AdSense account to the channel immediately after transfer. This means that revenue will be directed to your payment details without any waiting period. With the Basic package, there may be a short waiting period to change the AdSense account.

How much can I expect to earn with a pre-monetized channel?

Earnings vary widely depending on your content, niche, audience engagement, and how actively you upload new videos. Our channel listings include information about previous earnings when available, but future earnings will depend on how you manage and grow the channel.

What monetization features are available?

All our pre-monetized channels have access to the full suite of YouTube monetization features, including:

  • Ad revenue from display, overlay, and video ads
  • Channel memberships (if eligible based on subscriber count)
  • Super Chat and Super Stickers (for live streams)
  • Merchandise shelf (if eligible)
  • YouTube Premium revenue share

Specific eligibility for certain features may depend on the channel's subscriber count and region.
