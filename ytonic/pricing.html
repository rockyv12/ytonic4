Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pre-monetized YouTube channels

How do I know the channels are legitimate?

All our channels undergo a rigorous verification process to ensure they comply with YouTube's policies. We check monetization status, content history, and account standing before listing any channel. We also provide a secure escrow system for all transactions.

What's the difference between Basic and Premium packages?

The Basic package ($389) includes channels that meet the minimum monetization requirements with 1,000+ subscribers and 4,000+ watch hours. The Premium package ($449) offers channels with 1,500+ subscribers, 6,000+ watch hours, instant AdSense change capability, and priority support for a more advanced starting point.

What does "Instant AdSense Change" mean?

With our Premium package, you can immediately change the AdSense payment details to your own account, allowing you to start receiving revenue directly without any waiting period. The Basic package requires the standard YouTube waiting period (approximately 30 days) before you can change payment details.

What are Aged Channels and why are they so affordable?

Aged Channels are YouTube accounts that have been created at least 1 year ago but haven't yet reached monetization requirements. They're perfect for creators on a budget who want to skip the initial account setup and age requirements. They're priced at $20 because they require additional work to reach monetization status, but they give you a head start compared to creating a brand new channel.
